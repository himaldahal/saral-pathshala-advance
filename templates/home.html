About Saral Pathshala

Saral Pathshala (formerly known as Saral Shikshya) is one of Nepal's most prominent entrance exam preparation institutes, based in Bagbazar, Kathmandu. With years of experience preparing students for competitive university entrances, the institute combines expert faculty, innovative technology, and structured study materials to help every student succeed.

Exams We Cover

Saral Pathshala provides expert coaching for all major Nepal university entrance exams: IOE (Engineering), CEE (Medical/MBBS/BDS), BSc CSIT / BCA / BIT (IT & Computing), CMAT / KUUMAT (Management), and B.A.LL.B (Law). Both physical classroom and online modes are available with morning, afternoon, and evening shifts.

Online Entrance Prep Nepal

Our online platform offers concept-based video lectures, a 50,000+ MCQ bank, live computer-based mock tests, and detailed performance analytics | accessible from any device, anywhere in Nepal. New students receive free demo classes and complimentary mock tests upon registration.
